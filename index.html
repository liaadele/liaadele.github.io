Romeo and Juliet
A set of three posters to advertise a fictional production of Romeo and Juliet.

The process for these posters started with sketches on thick water color paper. Then, carefully, with an x-acto knife I removed layers of the paper until I was left with just the cardboard backing. This created a stencil of my drawings and I brushed india ink onto them and created multiple prints of each image. I then scanned them all in and used Photoshop to put everything together.

Ben Buelow's Tennis Lessons
A website to promote an up and coming tennis player giving lessons on the side.

The site's theme is meant to feel like a nice sunny day to inspire the visiter to want to get out and play! There are clouds in the sky, grass on the ground, and a big yellow tennis ball to represent the sun. The menue is the yellow of the tennis ball, and when you hover over the menu buttons the drop down and bounce like a tennis ball. The grass on the bottom is fixed, so even as you scroll down it is always there so you get the full picture of the sunny day.
